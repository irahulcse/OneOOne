What Value Do We Add?

Aplos, derived from the Greek roots of its name, is the personification simplicity and elegance. We offer an integrated solution that enables leadership at every level of the organization. Day - to - day visibility of the profitability based on real time company data is at your fingertips. Dashboards and reports will be generated in seconds for live data. We manage the technology at our in - house data center, protecting your confidentiality, privacy and accessibility. Where traditional digitization dedicates a core team to IT management, our process optimizes the overhead incurred by the business. We take care of infrastructure and interact with Power Users within the client company to deliver speedy support.

What’s Our Process?

Our process is a 5 step modular approach.
1. Business Re - Structuring
Based on our three corner stones of a sound business foundation, we create profit center entities. Entity creation is a process of its own: it involves a deep analysis of the company’s vision and how it manages its feasibility, liquidity and sustainability
2. Data Processing
Once the structural foundation is in place, involved collaboration with the client allows us to collect, clean and streamline the data that is absolutely critical to the company’s progress. The results of this stage are later used for aPOP validation
3. aPOP Configuration
This step is internal to Aplos, where we tailor fit our technology and prepare it for the client end - use. We strive to imbibe the client’s vision into the platform. Through the configuration, incorporate the 4 P’s (philosophy, policy, procedure and purpose) through the data integration.
4. Rolling Out
During this phase, we train and bring the service live over the network. The service seamlessly integrates into the user experience. It is brought live right up to the day before it launches, ready to hit the ground running.
5. Post Go Live Support
We generate results and evaluate client satisfaction. Iterating for improvements is built into our process. Any updates to the technology are automatically applied to the client service. We maintain interaction with the Power Users at the organization to ensure smooth running for a specified period.

Our Approach

When working with clients, we evaluate their:

1. Customer Focus
Organizations depend on their customers. Therefore, we strive to meet our customers’ needs and to exceed their expectations.
2. Leadership
Leaders establish unity of purpose and direction of the organization. They should create and maintain the internal environment in which people can become fully involved in achieving organization’s objectives.
3. Employee Engagement and Competence
People at all levels are key to the success of the organization and their full involvement enables their abilities to be used for the organization’s benefit.
4. Process Approach
A desired result is achieved more efficiently when processes are clear and optimized.
5. Improvement Mechanism
Continual improvement of the organization’s overall performance should be a permanent objective of the organization.
6. Informed Decision Mechanism
Effective decisions are based on the analysis of data and information.
7. Relationship Management
An organization and its vendors are interdependent and a mutually beneficial relationship enhances the value of the supply chain.
