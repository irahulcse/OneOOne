ERP Finance

Entities and position codes links elegantly with financial accounting. In accounting, there are four levels of organization. The Chart of Accounts (COA), the Budget Level, the Activity Level, and finally the individual Transactions. The COA is the overarching hierarchy of all transactions the whole organization can incur. For example, sales expenses are registered in the COA. In the budget level, this category is broken down into commercial expenses, sales commission, commercial expenses, conveyance expense etc.

Operational / Transactional Process

These are processes which define the primary activities that a company needs to perform in order to successfully execute its business.

Control Based Process

These processes get feedback from environment to deploy procedures to re-establish homeostasis. They reduce time lag since feedback allows preventive measures, real time responses to trigger signals.

Strategic Process

The top level process (requires high level intellectual capability) and has widespread effects in the structure and functioning of the organization.
